About

Hi, I’m Darryl. My passion lies in the connection between healthy eating and caring for our planet. While my personal diet often includes animal-based foods, I believe in mindful consumption for everyone.

Mindful Meals is about making conscious food choices that nourish both our bodies and the environment. Here, you’ll find simple, sustainable recipes and tips focused on appreciating ingredients, reducing waste, and making thoughtful decisions.

My goal is to make mindful eating accessible, encouraging everyone to enjoy delicious food while being mindful of our impact. Let’s explore how we can eat well and support a healthier planet together.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is “mindful eating”?

It involves slowing down, eating without distractions, and paying attention to your body’s hunger and fullness cues. It’s about cultivating a healthier and more conscious relationship with food.

Are the recipes on Mindful Meals strictly vegetarian or vegan?

While many of the recipes on Mindful Meals will feature plant-based ingredients to promote sustainability and accessibility, you may also find recipes that include animal products. The primary focus is on whole, unprocessed ingredients and mindful preparation, with an emphasis on reducing our environmental impact through our food choices.

We believe that healthy and responsible eating should be accessible to everyone.
